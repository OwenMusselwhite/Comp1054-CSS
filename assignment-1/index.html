About the Artist

Air Supply is an Australian soft rock duo formed by Russell Hitchcock and Graham Russell in the mid‑1970s. They are known for their smooth harmonies and emotional ballads, they became internationally successful throughout the 1980s with chart‑topping hits like "The One That You Love" and "All Out of Love". Their music is heartfelt, with great melodies, and a signature romantic style that is loved by audiences around the world.
